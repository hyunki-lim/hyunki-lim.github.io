Hyunki Lim

Hyunki Lim is currently an assistant professor in the Division of Computer Science and Engineering, Kyonggi Univ. (KGU) in Suwon, Korea. Prior to coming to KGU, he did his postdoc at KIST, and Ph.D., M.S. and B.S. at Chung-Ang Univ., Korea. His research interest includes advanced machine algorithms with innovative applications such as music emotion recognition and smart factory.
